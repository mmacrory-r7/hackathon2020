    Hear from the class of 2020

    Name: Rachel McAllister

    Job Title: Software Engineering Intern (Avengers)

    Course: Computer Science @ QUB

    Favourite Sport: Camogie

    What does your job entail?

    I work directly with senior developers on live Rapid7 products. Day to day I have been using command line and Jenkins. I also use intelliji and write in java.

    What is being an intern at Rapid7 like?

    Great! The culture is amazing; there are always fun activites planned. The people would go over and above to help you and guide you in the right direction - even if they have never met you before.

    What advice would you give to someone looking to be an intern at Rapid7?

    #BringYou. You need to have a good attitude to continuous learning - especially in software engineering as there is always something new.
